ABOUT ME


I have always been interested in the intersection between art and technology. I had decided to learn how to code and use that as my medium to create a new artistic narrative.


I am finishing up my last semester at NYU with a degree in Art History. This past summer I completed a tweleve week intensive web developement course at the New York Code + Design Academy. I learned to love front-end design and started creating applications that merged the tech and art world together.


When I am not coding, You can find me roaming the Impresionistic wing of the MoMA or at a coffee shop sipping on some cold brew. Art and technology are complimentary, and cannot be sepearated. Let's make something new and beautiful!
